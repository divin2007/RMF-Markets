Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common trader inquiries

RMF Escrow holds your payment in a secure neutral account until you confirm the receipt and quality of the goods. Only after the buyer's approval or the expiration of the inspection period are funds released to the seller. This ensures trust in high-value Rwandan marketplace transactions.
First, check the "Logistics" tab to see real-time GPS tracking. If the delay exceeds 48 hours without update, you can trigger a "Delay Notification" in the order panel which alerts our logistics supervisors to investigate the transit hub.
RMF negotiates flat-rate fees with major local telcos for trade-specific transfers. For amounts over 1,000,000 RWF, we recommend using the RMF Integrated Bank Transfer which offers lower fixed processing costs compared to tiered mobile money percentages.
Look for the blue "RMF Verified" badge on seller profiles. This badge indicates that we have verified their RDB registration, physical warehouse location, and tax compliance history in Rwanda.
